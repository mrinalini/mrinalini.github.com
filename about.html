Mrinalini Chandra is an award-winning jewellery/accessories designer. She acquired her Bachelor's degree in Design from NIFT, New Delhi, specializing in Fashion and Lifestyle Accessories. She then moved to Milan to earn her Master's degree in High Jewellery and Luxury Goods Design from Creative Academy, Milan, Italy. Mrinalini has worked with some of the best brands in the world such as Montblanc, Tanishq and Titan.
