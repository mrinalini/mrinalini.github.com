The Designer

With an urge to take handcrafted finesse to a new high, inspired by the the rich culture & poetic verses of India, Trained in Fashion luxury at the Creative Academy Milan & NIFT New Delhi. Mrinalini worked at Tanishq, New Delhi and Montblanc, Germany before launching her own label in India.

Working on custom projects relating to multiple facets of design, the designer is best known for her the striking unique ideas beautifully translated to three dimensional products. Her strength lies in handcrafting brilliance combined with modern aesthetics. Training the Fashion capital of the world, Milan, Italy has given an edge to her design aesthetics, Although, the aspect of the glorious rezendous with remains of history, from a very early age in India, always reflect in her work and expressions.

Mrinalini Chandra's creations can be defined as an eclectic range of whimsical, slightly modern slightly ancient, slightly stuck in time, high fashion jewellery couture and accessories.
